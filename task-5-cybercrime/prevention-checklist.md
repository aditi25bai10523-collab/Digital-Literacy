# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on suspicious or unknown links in emails or messages
- Always check the sender’s email address carefully
- Never share OTP, passwords, or personal details with anyone
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Always verify UPI ID and recipient name before making payments
- Do not scan unknown QR codes for receiving money
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or financial transactions
- Keep your devices and apps updated for better security

## Reporting Cybercrime

- Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
